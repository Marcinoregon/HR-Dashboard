The Big Idea

Why Data-Driven HR?

Mr. Macky's Cajun Cuisine operates 148 restaurant units with 6,559 employees. Managing this workforce by instinct alone isn't enough — we need evidence.

  • Turnover costs money. Over 39,000 employees have left. What's driving it?
  • Hiring must be fair. Are our selection processes equitable for all candidates?
  • Pay should reward value. Does experience actually predict performance?
  • Engagement matters. But does it translate to profit?

Module 4

Compensation & Value

Does experience equal performance? This module tracks the relationship between prior experience, wage rate, and actual job performance across 322 employees with complete data.

💡 The Paradox: Employees with 0 years of experience perform nearly as well as those with 10+ years — but earn significantly less. Should we rethink how we price experience?
